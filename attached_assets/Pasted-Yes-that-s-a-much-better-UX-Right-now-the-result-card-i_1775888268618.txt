## Best logic

For Free users:
- use a simple built-in rule:
  - up to 8 hours total driving in a day = no accom required
  - over 8 hours = recommend 1 night
  - over 16 hours = recommend 2 nights
  - over 24 hours = recommend 3 nights
- or if you want slightly more forgiving:
  - use 10 hours instead of 8

For Pro users:
- let them set a profile preference:
  - **Preferred max driving hours per day**
- then use that to calculate recommended nights

Example:
- total drive time = 17 hours
- preferred max driving hours/day = 8
- driving days needed = ceil(17 / 8) = 3
- recommended nights of accom = driving days - 1 = 2

That is smart and actually useful.

## 3. Accommodation recommendation based on driving time

Add logic to recommend accommodation nights based on total driving time.

### For Free users
Use a built-in default maximum driving time per day.

Recommended default:
- 8 hours/day
OR
- 10 hours/day

Use one default consistently.

### Recommended logic
Let:
- total_drive_hours = route travel time including return if selected
- max_drive_hours_per_day = default for Free, profile setting for Pro

Then:
- driving_days_needed = ceil(total_drive_hours / max_drive_hours_per_day)
- recommended_accommodation_nights = max(0, driving_days_needed - 1)

Examples:
- 6h total drive → 0 nights
- 9h total drive with 8h/day max → 1 night
- 17h total drive with 8h/day max → 2 nights

This recommendation should feel practical and realistic.
